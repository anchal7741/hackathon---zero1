Why Do We

Write blogs?

A lot of individuals start a blog to share their knowledge, Especially the curious types, as they understand the knowledge they have is precious, and they should document and share the same. And it makes sense, as one also learns more by teaching. Since blogging requires you to research more, and come up with the best version of your knowledge. This will also help legitimize your expertise and expand your online presence and platform. It also helps in promoting your credibility. It also allows a creative outlet for those who have become consumed by everyday life.
